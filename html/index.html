Yoga Dhyana is a practice that combines the principles of yoga and meditation. "Yoga" refers to a holistic discipline that encompasses physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation practices to promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Laughter Wellness is a practice that focuses on promoting laughter as a means to enhance well-being and overall health. It combines elements of laughter exercises, deep breathing techniques, and mindfulness to create a holistic approach to laughter. Laughter Wellness aims to harness the physical, mental, and emotional benefits of laughter in a structured and intentional way.

Talk therapy, also known as psychotherapy or counseling, is a form of therapeutic treatment that involves verbal communication between a trained therapist and an individual or group of individuals. It is a collaborative process aimed at addressing psychological and emotional challenges, improving mental health, and facilitating personal growth.

Sound meditation is a practice that involves using various sounds and vibrations as a focal point for meditation. It is a form of meditation that utilizes auditory stimuli to guide individuals into a state of deep relaxation, inner awareness, and heightened consciousness.

Child counseling, also known as child therapy or child psychotherapy, is a specialized form of therapy that focuses on providing mental health support and guidance to children and adolescents. It is a collaborative process between a trained therapist and a child, aimed at addressing emotional, behavioral, or social difficulties and promoting healthy development.

Doctor consultation refers to a professional medical service where individuals seek the expertise and guidance of a qualified medical doctor or physician. It involves an appointment or meeting with a doctor to discuss one's health concerns, receive a diagnosis, obtain medical advice, and explore treatment options.
